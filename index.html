    Participant Guide

  1. Show up, fill out RecWare & submit grades.
  2. Qualify against the Computer - Score is recorded (difficulty lowered until participant beats computer). Losing at easy results in disqualification.
  3. Participants play against person who qualified at similar level.
  4. Everyone gets Jamba Juice. Losers receive shuffle mp3 player.
  5. Winners are qualified for improved headphone bracket.
  6. Winners of headphone bracket are eligible to compete for nano mp3 player bracket next month, as long as they visit a park to load their school books as audio books onto their mp3 player.
