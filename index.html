iGreenTeam's Oakland Video Game Challenge

Fourth Friday: Play Games and Win Electronics

April 26, 2013 4-7pm at Mosswood Park

    Participant Guide

  1. Show up, fill out RecWare form & submit report card.
  2. Qualify against the Computer - Score is recorded (difficulty lowered until participant beats computer). Losing at easy results in disqualification.
  3. Participants play against person who qualified at similar level.
  4. Everyone gets Jamba Juice. Both players receive shuffle mp3 player.
  5. Winners are qualified for improved headphone bracket.
  6. Winners of headphone bracket receive headphones, and are eligible to compete for a nano mp3 player next month, as long as they complete next month eligibility.

    Skateboard Challenge

  1. Learn skateboard basics & tricks.
  2. Play S.K.A.T.E. for prizes (like H.O.R.S.E., but for skateboards).

    To Be Eligible for the Next Month

  1. Contact the person who recruited you.
  2. Submit your school reading assignments to them.
  3. Make an appointment with them to receive an SD card with your assignments as audio books.

    Recruiter Guide

  1. Recruit students with your marked fliers.
  2. Get prizes based on how many kids you recruit.
